❓ Frequently Asked Questions

💡 How do I convert CGPA to Percentage?

You can use our CGPA ➡ Percentage converter. Most universities follow CGPA × 9.5, but you can also set a custom scale in Settings.

📊 Can I calculate SGPA to CGPA?

Yes, just enter all your semester SGPA values in the SGPA to CGPA converter and get the average.

🧮 How is Attendance calculated?

Just enter total classes and attended classes. The tool shows your attendance percentage instantly.
